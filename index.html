Game Projects

In Darkness

Description

In Darkness is a single player horror game.
This game was created with Unity and Maya with a team of 4, where I participated as the game and level designer, 3D modeller and animator of the demon as well as menu design. Props such as furniture are taken from the unity store.

Gameplay

The player plays a little girl that has been trapped in the mansion and must escape before the demon catches her. To escape, the player has to find all candles (those are randomly placed each game) and blow them out. For this, a special implementation allows players to blow physically into their microphone to blow the candles out. This will break the spell that is put on the front door.
The player can also use crosses to scare the demon away. This gives the player more time, however, the crosses are limited and only once usable.

Wired

Description

Wired is a game created during the Retro Game Jam 2019, a 48-hour competition where teams of students develop a complete game. The theme for this competition was "Cyborg and Humans" as of honour for our special guest Michael Pondsmith, the author of Cyberpunk.

Gameplay

Wired is a pixel speed run game, where the player can choose between a human or cyborg. The human runs faster but the cyborg can jump higher. The game has three levels with increasing difficulty and speed. I designed the Pixel characters with Aesprite where I also did their animations and some of the obstacles. All levels where designed using Unity.

Description

Pacman Diamond Rush

Description

Pacman Diamond Rush was an AI project and the task was to create an AI script for the ghosts. For this my team decided to programm the ghost to wander around randomly and then persue Pacman when he is close enough.
To give it some variety, one ghost has a different code and tries to persue the player directly to give the player more stress. The game was build in Unity and the ghost,diamonds and pacman are modelled in Maya and the settings where taken from the Unity Store. Gameplay: You play as Pacman and need to collect at least 100 diamonds (not all) to get to the next level. There are three levels: Grassland, Farm, and Castle. If you get caught by the ghost, you spawn at your starting point. If you die 3 times then your game is over!

iKey

Description

iKey is a concept that was created for the IoT hackathon at JCU. It is a concept for a keyless doorsystem that ensures safety using face recognition and voice recognition.

By using an app, clients can open doors by speaking the password into the app that detect voices. Furthermore, a camera installed at the front door can recognize the person's face. We used a raspberry pie connected with a camera to demonstrate the face recognition. If the face and voice matches, it will allow the person inside the house. The app has also a live stream and notifies the owner if someone comes home or if a unrecognized face is detected. Besides using the app, the client can connect the iKey system with a fitbit that tracks the location of the wearer. If the location and face matches, then access is granted.

Activities

Hackathon

Hackathon Participation in 2009 at James Cook University.


I participated in the Hackathon with three other students from James Cook University and won the first prize with our iKey Concept.
This Hackathon was a 48hr challenge with the theme "Internet of Things".

For the theme we decided to create an app called iKey that offers a better door security system. I created an app in Android Studio (first time using this tool!) and added google voice recogntion, so that the user can say the password and unlock the door. The app will write out the word on the display and then check if the password is correct. If the password is correct, the lock icon on the app with change to an open lock image.
